Bollywood ki boli ft. Pune

Agricultural College Of Pune

The comically acclaimed Sanjay Dutt starrer film, Munna Bhai MBBS was largely shot at the Agricultural College of Pune. From the grand exterior to the classrooms there’s a good chunk of Pune embedded in the film, bas kya bhidu?

11 East Street

The movie that was worked as a kick-starter for a real-life romance between Ranbir and Katrina, also shot some key moments in Pune. Especially the sequence from the song ‘Prem Ki Naiyya’ was shot at our very own 11 East Street Cafe. It’s hard to miss considering how obsessed people were back then with this movie and the songs.

F.C Road

At least 90% of the film is shot in Pune right from MG Road, Shisha Jazz Cafe, Magarpatta, F.C Road, Koregaon Park and Peth areas. How can one forget Cafe Goodluck and of course the Khadki underpass where Tabu gets kidnapped.

Symbiosis Lavale Campus

If you haven’t been living under a rock, you must be familiar that the 2011 hit, ‘Bodyguard’ was shot at Symbiosis Lavale Campus. What most people do not know of is that couple of scenes from ‘Dangal’ were shot here too.
